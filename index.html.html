Introduction

About us



Silver Riders Cycling Club are open one evening per week and both Saturday and Sundays at weekends, and they organise events and rides for a range of abilities, covering different routes and difficulty of rides. The club has a calendar of events and there are 3 different groups to choose from. The fast group start at 9.15am promptly on a Sunday morning and cover on average 50 miles, the medium paced group start at 9.30am and cover on average 35 miles. The slower pace group leave at 10.00am and cover on average 25 miles. All groups meet up for lunch at a local pub or eating place. New riders are always welcome.
